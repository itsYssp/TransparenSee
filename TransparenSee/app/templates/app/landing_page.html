Features

Everything you need for
financial transparency

Built specifically for academic student organizations that need accountability, traceability, and trust.

Blockchain Records

Every transaction is hashed and stored on-chain — immutable, tamper-proof, and permanently verifiable by anyone.

Multi-Level Approval

Transactions flow through Treasurer → Auditor → President → Adviser before being finalized, ensuring accountability at every step.

Anomaly Flagging

Auditors can flag suspicious transactions and reports for further review — keeping your organization's finances clean.

Full Audit Trail

Every action by every user is logged — who did what, when, and why. Nothing is ever hidden.

Society Fee Tracking

Track student society fee payments per academic year — paid, unpaid, or partial — with real-time collection status.

Community Chat

Global chat for all officers,advisers, and campus admins plus organization-level announcements — keeping everyone informed and connected.
